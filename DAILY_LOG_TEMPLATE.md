## Tips for Effective Logging

1. **Be Consistent:** Log daily, even if brief
2. **Be Honest:** Document struggles, not just wins
3. **Be Specific:** "Learned CNNs" < "Implemented ResNet50 and achieved 95% accuracy"
4. **Include Code:** Link to actual implementations
5. **Add Visuals:** Screenshots, plots, diagrams
6. **Reflect:** Don't just list, think about *why*
7. **Review Regularly:** Weekly and monthly reviews are crucial
8. **Keep It Simple:** Don't let logging take more than 10-15 minutes
9. **Make It Yours:** Customize this template to fit your style
10. **Share Progress:** Consider making logs public for accountability
